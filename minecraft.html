Your one stop shop for detailed infomation on any minecraft Mobs

Hostile mobs in Minecraft are aggressive non-player characters (NPCs) that attack the player on sight or when they are within their detection range. They are also known as monsters and usually spawn in the dark.

Passive mobs are gentle creatures that have no quarrel with you. Their reaction to a player might vary (some may run away if they spot you) but the common denominator for this group of mobs is that they will never attack you even if you were to attack them.
